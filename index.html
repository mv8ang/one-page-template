About

Tobias Hilmer (a.k.a DJ Hilto) was born on the 26th of August in Brasschaat (In Antwerp). He never learned any instruments but was always fascinated by music. Over the years he listened to all sort of music. Going from Rock music to Pop music, house music, hardstyle...

This all is because of his father. He started recording and making 'radioshows' from a young age. Everytime the radio was on, Tobias asked a lot of questions about who made the song, what they were saying, how old it was etc.

Because of this, he came in contact with a lot of different genres. Over the years he started listening to the music he loved the most, house music. From there on, everything started.

Biography

At the end of 2014, Antwerp based youngster (17 y/o), Tobias Hilmer, Better known as DJ Hilto, started following his dreams.

It all started by making mashups and edits. He started doing this in Sony Vegas 10 but not a long time later he decided to start using Fruity Loops. Withing this DAW he continued making this mashups.

By contacting other people across the globe he started making mashups in collaboration with people frm a lot of different countries like Italy, Mexico, U.S.A...

After doing this for a few months, he contacted a local youth club in Brasschaat and they were willing to give him a chance. They've put him on the line up of "Crisisfuif - Halloween Edition". After his set he knew that this was what he wanted. He wanted to perform in front of other people, let them have a good time and let everyone enjoy some fresh music.

Some time later he played again at "Crisisfuif". Between his sets he keeps practising to improve is technic and skill. He has always loved music, and he always will.
